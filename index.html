Meet the Founder

"Growing up in a rural community, I always wanted to bring I.T. services that were simple and affordable to my friends and neighbors, with that being said, I decided to launch my own small business/project, and thats where Tech Center Support Service started."

I grew up in Rayville, Missouri and graduated from Richmond High School in 2025. While I was in high school, I attended the Lex La-Ray Technical Center where I received my PC Pro Certification and Competency In Networking Certification while being in the Computer Maintenance and Networking program.

I was also an active member of the Richmond FFA. I served on two officer teams, competed in the Agricultural Communications Career Development Event at the State and National level, and have had several experiences that has shaped me into the person I am today. I am also a member of the Old New Garden Primitive Baptist Church. I currently work as a Technology Support Technician for the Excelsior Springs School District.
